About Sbarro Aruba


Tradition since 1956

The original chain started in Brooklyn New York and was founded by Gennaro and Camela Sbarro. These two only believed in serving the best Italian dishes to their guests. From a local Italian grocery store, to a leading authentic New York Style pizza chain with 800+ branches in several locations. We only use the freshest ingredients.

Sbarro was brought to the island of Aruba in the year 2000 by the van Romondt family. They continued the tradition of handmade pizzas and pastas for 20 years. Sbarro is located in three areas on the island of Aruba, and everybody who works there will be happy to take your order.
